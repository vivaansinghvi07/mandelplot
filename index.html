Information
To use this, click anywhere on the screen to zoom into the graph at that point.

You can also set custom bounds, and the "Click Location" shows the coordinates of your click.

By using the "Apply Settings" button, you can reset the number of workers, resolution, and color scheme without affecting the graph's zoom.

To undo a zoom, you can use the "Undo Zoom" button. You can do this as many times as you want. However, resizing the window or setting custom bounds clears your zoom history.
